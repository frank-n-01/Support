# Mirror Talk - Privacy Policy

## Information We Collect

Mirror Talk does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data you enter in the app stays on your device.

## iCloud Sync

Mirror Talk uses Apple's iCloud service to sync your conversation history across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). The developer does not have access to your iCloud data.

## Third-Party Services

Mirror Talk does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Mirror Talk does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated along with app updates. Changes will be posted on this page.

This policy is effective as of 2026-04-02.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/frank-n-01/Support/issues).
