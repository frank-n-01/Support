Communicate Clearly. Anytime, Anywhere.
Mirror Talk is a simple yet powerful app for smooth face-to-face communication through writing.

◉ Key Features
・Switch between Keyboard and Handwriting Modes
　Type detailed messages with the keyboard or quickly jot down simple notes by hand—choose the input method that suits the moment.

・Automatic Conversation History
　All messages are saved automatically, so you can easily revisit past conversations when needed.

・Customizable Fonts, Colors, and Backgrounds
　Personalize the look and feel of your messages to match your style and ensure readability.

・iCloud Sync
　Keep your conversation history safe and in sync across your devices.

◉ MirrorTalk Unlimited (In-App Purchase)
Enjoy unlimited written communication.
The free version includes up to 1,000 interactions (each tap of the "x" delete button counts as one interaction).
