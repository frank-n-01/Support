# ZeroOneFlow - Privacy Policy

## Information We Collect

ZeroOneFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app works entirely offline with no network communication.

## Third-Party Services

ZeroOneFlow does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

ZeroOneFlow does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
